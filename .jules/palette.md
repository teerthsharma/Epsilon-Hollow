## 2024-05-26 - Adding Empty States for Interactive Streams
**Learning:** In interactive, stream-based UI components like `ChatInterface`, starting with a completely blank screen causes confusion about system state. Without guidance, users may not know they can initiate interaction.
**Action:** Always provide a visually distinct "empty state" that confirms the system is online and provides a clear call-to-action on how to start (e.g., "Inject knowledge into the stream to begin.").
